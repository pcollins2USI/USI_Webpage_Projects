Your Mental Game

Maintain Focus

Don't Broadcast Your Weaknesses

If you consistently miss a particular shot, don't complain about it while playing. Your opponent may pick up on that weakness and deliver the same shot over and over.

Control Your Breathing

  • Breathe in through your nose and exhale through your mouth.
  • Time your breathing so that you exhale when connecting with the ball.
